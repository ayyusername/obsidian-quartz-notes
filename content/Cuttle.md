21 Points to Win

A: scrap all point cards on the field
2: Either--
	Counter target one-off effect
	Scrap target royal
3: Choose a card in the scrap pile and add to your hand
4: Your opponent discards (2) cards of their choosing
5: Draw (2) cards from the deck. Do not exceed (8) card limit!
6: Scrap all royals + "Glasses 8s" on the field. 
7: Choose one of the top two cards from the deck. Play however you choose. Discard other. 
8: Glasses!
9: Return a card from your opponents field to their hand. They cannot play it next turn. 

Royals: 

K: Reduce points to win
	21 > 14 > 10 > 7 > 5
Q: Protect other cards from one-offs
	Scuttling still allowed!
J: Steal a point card. 
	Stays on board. If discarded, point returns to opponents board.
