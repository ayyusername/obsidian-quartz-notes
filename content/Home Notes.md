
#### In the Kitchen: 

##### When cooking something in the Instant Pot: 

**Instructions:** 

Here are some easy to follow instructions. Please ignore all data belore "Scratch Sheet". 

Template: 

Ingredient // # Minutes at HIGH Pressure in Instant Pot // 1:X:Y 1 part beans : x parts water : y tsp salt

For Example: 

Chickpeas // 45 // 15 // 1:3:1
Means: Cook 1 cup of chickpeas in 3 cups of water with 1 tsp of salt on high pressure for 45 minutes in the instant pot. Allow a 15 minute natural release before removing the lid.
