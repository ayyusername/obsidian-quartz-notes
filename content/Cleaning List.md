

## Weekly Tasks

### Bedroom
- Change the bedsheets

### Bathroom
- Clean the toilet
- Wash the sink
- Mop the floor

### Kitchen
- Wipe down all exterior surfaces with hot water and a rag

### Laundry
- Wash Audrey's laundry

## Monthly Tasks

### Coffee Maker
1. Run a cleaning cycle with 1 cup of vinegar
2. Run 2 additional cycles with clean water

### Dishwasher
- Run a heavy cycle with 1 cup of vinegar

### Washing Machine
- Run a cleaning cycle with 1 cup of bleach on a hot setting

### Fridge
- **Deep Clean:**
  - Pull out all items and wipe down the interior
  - Wipe down shelves with hot water, removing any items as needed for a thorough clean
  - Deep clean all drawers using a dilute vinegar spray

### Microwave
1. Moisten a bar rag and heat in the microwave for 5 minutes on high
2. Remove the dish and spinner
3. Wipe out all surfaces
4. Wipe with a hot towel
5. Return the cleaned dish and spinner

### Dusting
- Vacuum up dust from all surfaces
- Wipe down surfaces with a hot rag

## Notes
- When washing laundry, do not use more than 2 tablespoons of laundry detergent.
