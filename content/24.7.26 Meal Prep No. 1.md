## Ingredient List

- Carrots: 
  - 3 cups julienned (Marinated Vegetable Salad)
  - 1 large, peeled and grated (Curtido)
- Zucchini: 2 cups julienned (Marinated Vegetable Salad)
- Red onion: 
  - 1 cup vertically sliced (julienne-cut) (Marinated Vegetable Salad)
  - 1 small, thinly sliced (Curtido)
  - 1/2, finely diced (about 3/4 cup) (Cowboy Caviar)
- Red bell pepper: 
  - 1/2 cup julienned (Marinated Vegetable Salad)
  - 1 whole, seeded and finely diced (Cowboy Caviar)
- Fresh parsley: 2 tablespoons chopped (Marinated Vegetable Salad)
- Garlic: 3-4 cloves, minced (Cowboy Caviar)
- Plum tomatoes: 3, cored, seeded if desired, and diced (Cowboy Caviar)
- Corn: 1 1/2 cups fresh kernels or thawed frozen (Cowboy Caviar)
- Jalapeño: 1, seeded and finely diced (Cowboy Caviar)
- Cilantro: 1/2 cup chopped leaves and tender stems, plus extra for garnish (Cowboy Caviar)
- Scallion: 1, white and green parts chopped (optional, for Cowboy Caviar garnish)
- Broccoli: 1 1/2 pounds (about 2 medium heads), stalks and florets prepared as described (Italian Broccoli Salad)
- Shallot: 1 small, thinly sliced (Italian Broccoli Salad)

### Canned Goods
- Black beans: 1 (15-ounce) can, rinsed (Cowboy Caviar)
- Black-eyed peas: 1 (15-ounce) can, rinsed (Cowboy Caviar)

### Dairy
- Pecorino Romano or Parmesan cheese: 2 tablespoons grated (Marinated Vegetable Salad)
- Provolone: 3 slices (about 3 ounces), torn or cut into bite-size pieces (Italian Broccoli Salad)

### Dry Goods
- Red wine vinegar:

  - 1/4 cup (Cowboy Caviar)

  - 1/3 cup (Cowboy Caviar)
  - 1/3 cup (Italian Broccoli Salad)

- Dried oregano:

- Apple cider vinegar: 1/2 cup (Curtido)

## Prep List and Timeline

### 0:00 - 0:30
1. Wash all produce.
2. Julienne 3 cups carrots, 2 cups zucchini, 1/2 cup red bell pepper, and 1 cup red onion.
3. Grate 1 large carrot for Curtido.
4. Thinly slice 1 small head of cabbage and 1 small red onion for Curtido.
5. Chop 2 tablespoons fresh parsley.

### 0:30 - 1:00
6. Prepare Marinated Vegetable Salad dressing:
   - In a small bowl, whisk together 1/4 cup red wine vinegar, 2 tbsp grated cheese, 2 tbsp chopped parsley, 1 tbsp olive oil, 1 tbsp water, 1/4 tsp dried basil, 1/4 tsp dried oregano, 1/4 tsp salt, 1/8 tsp black pepper.

7. Start Marinated Vegetable Salad:
   - Microwave julienned carrots for 1.5 minutes, set aside to cool.
   - Microwave julienned zucchini for 1.5 minutes, set aside to cool.

8. Start Curtido:
   - In a small saucepan, combine 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar, 1/2 cup water, 1 tsp dried oregano, 1/2 tsp ground cumin, 1/2 tsp black pepper, 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes, 1 tsp sugar, and 1 1/2 tsp kosher salt.
   - Bring to a boil, stirring to dissolve sugar and salt.

### 1:00 - 1:30
9. Finish Marinated Vegetable Salad:
   - Combine cooled carrots, zucchini, julienned onion, and bell pepper in a large bowl.
   - Pour dressing over vegetables and toss to coat.
   - Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate.

10. Finish Curtido:
    - In a large bowl, combine sliced cabbage, grated carrot, and sliced onion.
    - Pour hot vinegar mixture over vegetables and mix thoroughly.
    - Pack into a large jar, pressing down firmly. Add water if needed to submerge vegetables.
    - Let sit at room temperature for 2 hours before refrigerating.

11. Prep for Cowboy Caviar:
    - Mince 3-4 garlic cloves.
    - Dice 3 plum tomatoes.
    - Finely dice 1/2 red onion.
    - Drain and rinse black beans and black-eyed peas.
    - If using fresh corn, cut kernels from cobs to measure 1 1/2 cups.
    - Seed and finely dice 1 bell pepper and 1 jalapeño.
    - Chop 1/2 cup cilantro.

### 1:30 - 2:00
12. Assemble Cowboy Caviar:
    - Make dressing: Whisk 1/3 cup olive oil, 1/4 cup red wine vinegar, minced garlic, 1 tsp sugar, 1/2 tsp salt, and 1/2 tsp pepper in a medium bowl.
    - Add prepared vegetables, beans, and cilantro. Toss to combine.
    - Cover and refrigerate.

13. Prep for Italian Broccoli Salad:
    - Thinly slice 1 small shallot, rinse under cold water, and transfer to a large bowl.
    - Prepare 1 1/2 pounds broccoli:
      - Cut stalks from heads.
      - Peel fibrous outer layer from stalks.
      - Thinly slice stalks crosswise.
      - Slice heads into bite-size pieces.

### 2:00 - 2:30
14. Finish Italian Broccoli Salad:
    - Add prepared broccoli to the bowl with shallots.
    - Season with salt and pepper.
    - Coarsely chop 1/2 cup roasted, salted almonds.
    - Add almonds, 1/4 cup sliced pickled peppers, 1/4 cup torn olives, 1/3 cup olive oil, 1/3 cup red wine vinegar to the bowl.
    - Tear or cut 3 slices of provolone into bite-size pieces and add to the bowl.
    - Toss vigorously until everything is combined.
    - Taste and adjust seasonings.

### 2:30 - 3:00
15. Final steps:
    - Review all prepared dishes.
    - Adjust seasonings if necessary.
    - Ensure all containers are properly sealed.
    - Clean up workspace and put away all equipment.

## Storage Instructions

1. Marinated Vegetable Salad: Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3-4 days.
2. Curtido: Store in a sealed jar in the refrigerator for up to 2 weeks. Flavor improves with time.
3. Cowboy Caviar: Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.
4. Italian Broccoli Salad: Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 4 days.

## Serving Instructions

1. Marinated Vegetable Salad: Toss before serving. Serve cold.
2. Curtido: Drain excess liquid before serving if desired.
3. Cowboy Caviar: Toss well before serving. Serve with tortilla chips.
4. Italian Broccoli Salad: Bring to room temperature before serving. Adjust seasoning if needed.

## Professional Tips

1. For julienning vegetables, use a mandoline if available for more consistent cuts and to save time.
2. When microwaving vegetables, arrange them in a single layer for even cooking.
3. For Cowboy Caviar, you can use frozen corn without thawing for a fresher taste and texture.
4. In the Italian Broccoli Salad, vary the size and shape of broccoli pieces for interesting texture.
5. Taste and adjust seasonings before final storage, as flavors can change after chilling.
