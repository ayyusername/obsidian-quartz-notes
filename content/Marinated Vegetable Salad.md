# Marinated Vegetable Salad

This marinated vegetable salad has a slight crunch and a tangy red wine vinaigrette that soaks into the veggies, making this dish nothing short of tastylicious.

## Ingredients

### Salad
- 3 cups julienne-cut carrots
- 2 cups julienne-cut zucchini (skin on if preferred)
- 1 cup vertically sliced red onion (julienne-cut)
- 1/2 cup julienne-cut red [[Atlas/210 Recipes/Ingredients/Bell Pepper|bell pepper]] (seeded)

### Dressing
- 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
- 2 tablespoons finely grated fresh Pecorino Romano or Parmesan cheese
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 tablespoon water
- 1/4 teaspoon dried basil
- 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Place carrots in a microwave-safe dish, cover with plastic wrap, and microwave on HIGH for 1 1/2 minutes, or until crisp-tender. Remove from microwave and plastic wrap. Set aside to cool.
2. Repeat step 1 with zucchini.
3. In a large bowl, combine carrots, zucchini, onion, and [[Atlas/210 Recipes/Ingredients/Bell Pepper|bell pepper]]. Toss together.
4. In a small bowl, combine all dressing ingredients and whisk together.
5. Pour dressing over vegetables and toss to coat.
6. Cover salad with plastic wrap and marinate in refrigerator for 2-3 hours, tossing once halfway through marinating if possible.
7. Toss again before serving and serve cold.

## Notes

- This dish can be made ahead of time, but wait until the day of serving to toss it with the dressing.
