### Zaalouk with Tahini
**Yotam Ottolenghi, NY Times**

**Ingredients:**

- 2 large red bell peppers
- 2 large eggplants
- 4 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 Tbsp tomato paste
- 2 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp sweet paprika
- 1 tsp Aleppo pepper flakes
- 2 large tomatoes, puréed
- ¼ cup parsley
- ¼ cup cilantro
- ½ tsp sugar
- 2 Tbsp lemon juice
- Scant ¼ cup tahini

**Directions:**

1. Roast peppers for 25-30 minutes and eggplants for 50 minutes at 450°F.
2. Sauté garlic with spices, add puréed tomatoes, parsley, cilantro, and sugar. Simmer for 6 minutes.
3. Add roasted peppers and eggplants. Cook for 3 minutes.
4. Drizzle with tahini sauce and serve with pitas.

### White Bean Salad with Roasted Cauliflower
**David Tanis, NY Times**

**Ingredients:**

- 1 small head cauliflower
- 4 cups cooked white beans
- 1 cup sliced celery heart
- 3 Tbsp chopped parsley
- 3 Tbsp slivered scallions

**For the dressing:**

- 1 large shallot
- 2 tsp Dijon mustard
- 1 Tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tsp lemon zest
- 2 Tbsp red wine vinegar
- ¼ cup olive oil

**Directions:**

1. Roast cauliflower at 400°F for 20-25 minutes.
2. Make the dressing by whisking together ingredients.
3. Toss beans, cauliflower, celery, and dressing. Let rest for 30 minutes.
4. Add parsley and scallions before serving.
